The Harmony Collaboration was inspired by a pressing need to build better relationships and broaden respectful dialogue between Indigenous and non-Indigenous communities by creating positive social change through the power of the performing arts. Together, the Harmony Collaboration and the Mississaugas of the Credit First Nation explored new ways to connect non-Indigenous Canadians to Indigenous culture and heritage.
